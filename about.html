Who we are.

Started in 2022, in St. Louis.

We started right here in the St. Louis area in 2022 with the goal of actually creating something meant to be a joke online. We found that a "joot," a boot made with denim material, would be a nice twist on current fashion trends. So, we did just that and created Joots. Now, we have one employee making everything behind Joots, the fast shipping, durable and fashionable jean boots, and the humour.


We wanted something indestructable, but fashionable

We hated how shoes of today fall apart so quickly for the cost of fashion, or a shoe basically indestructable but doesn't look good. So we challenged that. With Joots we were able to create fashionable boots in differing colours (soon!) and also have them hard to break down through daily use. Not really sure how we did it, but we did.
