Joots.

For everyone.

Everyone's favourite material.

"Joots" is a portmanteau of the words "jean" and "boots". They are boots made with denim materials, like your average blue jeans.

Helping the environment.

Joots are made from jeans found in donation bins that are too ripped and destroyed for wearing. We use these and reconstruct a boot from them, giving the jeans another chance at life. All jeans used to create Joots are put through a vigerous wash to remove bacteria but also to keep their structure.

Stylish for every season.

Our Joots are perfect for every season, whether it's 105 degrees outside or 30 degrees. Every pair of Joots is treated in a water-resistant coating to keep them nice and clean for a long time. You also enjoy the warmth of Joots during the winter, and the breathability during the summer.

Ultra comfortable.

Boots shouldn't be uncomfortable. Joots are padded on the inside with a breathable, synthetic fabric to maximum comfort and sweat resistance.
